# Code of Conduct

apsolut-cortex follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We expect everyone interacting with this project — in issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other project space — to act in a way that contributes to an open, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for all participants.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, harassment, or insulting/derogatory comments.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption of discussions, or spam.
- Publishing other people's private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Concerns can be reported to the maintainer privately — see the disclosure address in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) (the same private channel works for code-of-conduct concerns). Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially. Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban anyone for behaviors deemed inappropriate.

For the full text and the formal escalation guidelines, see the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
